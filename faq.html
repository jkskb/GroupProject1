Why "Disaster Source"?
We've created this site to provide crowd-sourced business status information during times when statuses are changing faster than standard technology sources are able to update.

Living in an area of the country that has experienced a number of hurricanes, we are aware of the need for immediate information building up to, during and immediately following a storm. In these times, the usual sources for shop hours become unreliable. Additionally, this is not a time when you can count on water, gas or food to be in stock. We also find that during and immediately following a storm that there is a desire to get out of the house, even if it's just to wander around the grocery store. While you want to get our, leaving your home and just wandering isn't safe nor practicle.
Where do you get your information?
We get our information from you! Everything we are providing is crowdsourced. It's just one more way we can come together as a community in a time of need.
Is the information accurate?
We are relying in our users to provide the best information and most information available. While we can't guarantee accuracy, we hope that our users find this access to information as helpful as we do and that will provide the motivation to update as accuratly.

Keep in mind, during disaster a situation statuses are changing rapidly. Look at the timestamp on the comments. We've provided that to help with your decision making.
Help! This information provided is incorrect for the store I'm at...
Please, return to our homepage and click on "Submit Business Update". This will pull up a form, which you can fill out with the current business status. The comments you provide will be added to the previous comments. We keep the comments displayed in sequential order, with the most recent appearing first.
What information should I provide?
Provide any information that you would find helpful. It never hurts to try to get information from an employee...Current hours? Do they have water in stock? What about beer!!?? What time do they plan on opening tomorrow? When is the next delivery of gas expected?
